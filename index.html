THE GLOBAL GDP RATE AND UNEMPLOYMENT
RATE AS AFFECTED BY THE COVID-19 PANDEMIC

As we all know, the COVID-19 Pandemic caused a great economic turmoil throughout the world. With this project, we aim to learn more about the effect of the said pandemic to the overall world economy. The data set that we have gathered includes both Quantitative and Qualitative Data. We have the Monthly Unemployment Rate and quarterly GDP Growth rate as our Quantitative Variables, while we have the Monthly Unemployment Rate Change and quarterly GDP Growth rate change as the Qualitative Variables.
